How to Make a Perfect Bruschetta

Bruschetta is a classic Italian appetizer that is easy to make at home.
Toasted bread is topped with tomatoes, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and fresh basil.
Use a high-quality balsamic vinegar for best results.
  • 1 loaf of French bread, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 8 diced roma (plum) tomatoes
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 ounce Parmesan, freshly grated
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of good quality balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps to do Bruschetta

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  3. Brush bread slices on both sides lightly with 1 tablespoon oil and place on large baking sheet. Toast bread until golden, 5 to 10 minutes, turning halfway through.
  4. Meanwhile, toss together tomatoes, basil, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in a bowl
  5. Mix in balsamic vinegar, 2 teaspoons olive oil, kosher salt, and pepper
  6. Spoon tomato mixture onto toasted bread slices.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
